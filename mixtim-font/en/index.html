Introducing “miXTim” –a modern geometric typeface, inspired by elegance. Designed with clean and stylized contemporary geometry. «miXTim» is a type system with high refined curves which focus on Clarity & Legibility.

 Leer en español

Type families are groups of fonts united by similar characters, but with their own features that differentiate them. They come in different thickness and width.

The “miXTim” family includes three weights (Thin, Regular and Bold) and covers several languages based on the Latin alphabet.

The subset of miXTim Bold in this kit supports the following languages:
Albanian, Basque, Breton, Chamorro, Danish, English, Faroese, Finnish, Frisian, Galician, German, Icelandic, Italian, Malagasy, Norwegian, Portuguese, Spanish, Alsatian, Aragonese, Arapaho, Arrernte, Asturian, Aymara, Bislama, Cebuano, Corsican, Fijian, French_creole, Genoese, Gilbertese, Greenlandic, Haitian_creole, Hiligaynon, Hmong, Hopi, Ibanag, Iloko_ilokano, Indonesian, Interglossa_glosa, Interlingua, Irish_gaelic, Jerriais, Lojban, Lombard, Luxembourgeois, Manx, Mohawk, Norfolk_pitcairnese, Occitan, Oromo, Pangasinan, Papiamento, Piedmontese, Potawatomi, Rhaeto-romance, Romansh, Rotokas, Sami_lule, Sardinian, Scots_gaelic, Seychelles_creole, Shona, Sicilian, Somali, Southern_ndebele, Swahili, Swati_swazi, Tagalog_filipino_pilipino, Tetum, Tok_pisin, Uyghur_latinized, Volapuk, Walloon, Warlpiri, Xhosa, Yapese, Zulu, Latinbasic, Ubasic

This font is available to download in two formats: Regular and Bold.
You are free to use it as you want within your projects with the SIL license, both personal and commercial.

What's included?

  • Files: mixtim-regular
  • Format files: TTF, EOT, SVG, WOFF, WOFF2
  • 1 weight: Regular (Bold and Thin on demand)
  • Multiple Language supported
